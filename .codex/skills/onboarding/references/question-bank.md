# Question Bank

Ask one question at a time. Do not use menus or reply codes.

Start the interview like this:

```md
**Your Interview Starts Here**

I am going to ask a few open questions, one at a time. Rambling is welcome; I will do the cleanup.

**First Question For You**
```

Core questions:

- `What did I misunderstand or underweight about your role, your work, or the people around you?`
- `What projects or workstreams are active right now, paused, background, or safe to ignore unless you bring them up?`
- `What feels most important over the next few days or weeks?`
- `What usually creates stress, dropped balls, or mental overhead?`
- `Who should I learn especially well, and what should I understand about how you work with them?`
- `Which tools should I have connected to be useful here, and what feels missing?`

After each answer:

- say what changed in your understanding
- reread connected context when useful
- ask the next still-missing question

Save durable memory only after the user has calibrated the map and only through a real memory or vault surface.
